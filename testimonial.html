Monuments

Singapore

Rizal made four trips to Singapore. His first overseas trip lasted two days in 1882, during which he took a sightseeing tour of the city of Singapore.

Spain

Madre España deserves to have her son's monument in the Philippines. For most of his travels in Europe, which lasted from 1882 to 1891, Rizal resided in Madrid.

Japan

Monument in Hibiya Park, Tokyo, Japan

When Rizal got to Yokahoma on February 18, 1888, he checked into the opulent Tokyo Hotel. The following day, he moved on to the Tokyo Hotel, where he was housed from March 2 to March 7. After that, he was sent to the Spanish Legation.

Bust in Orlando, Florida

Juneau head statue, Manila Square in Alaska

Herman Park, Houston, Texas

Eight US towns have Rizal monuments: Juneau, Alaska; Kauai and Lihue in Hawaii; Chicago, Illinois; Orlando, Florida; California; Cherry Hill in New Jersey; New York City; and Seattle, according to a story in the Asian Journal.
