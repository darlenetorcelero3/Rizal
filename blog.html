JOSE RIZAL FAMILY

FRANCISCO MERCADO

Francisco Engracio Rizal Mercado y Alejandro was the father of the Philippines' national hero Jose Rizal.

TEODORA ALONSO

Teodora Alonso Realonda y Quintos, She was best known as the mother of the Philippines' national hero Jose Rizal.

SATURNINA RIZAL

Saturnina Rizal Mercado de Hidalgo, was the eldest sister of Philippine national hero José Rizal.

PACIANO RIZAL

Paciano Rizal, the older brother of José Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines.

NARCISA RIZAL

Narcisa Rizal-Lopez was the third child and the third sister of the national hero Jose Rizal.

OLYMPIA RIZAL

Olympia Rizal was the fourth child and the sister of Jose Rizal, the Filipino national hero.

LUCIA RIZAL

Lucia Rizal is the fifth child of the Rizal family and the widow of Mariano Herbosa.

MARIA RIZAL

Maria was the sixth child in the family. She married Daniel Faustino Cruz of Biñan, Laguna.

JOSE RIZAL

José Protasio Rizal he is considered a national hero of the Philippines.

CONCEPCION RIZAL

Concepcion Rizal, also called 'Concha' by her siblings, was the eight child of the Rizal family. She died at the age of three.

JOSEFA RIZAL

Josefa Rizal's, or Panggoy, was the ninth child in the family and a member of the Katipunan..

TRINIDAD MERCADO

Trinidad Rizal, was a Filipina feminist leader and co-founder of the Philippines' first feminist organization, the Asociación Femenista Filipina.

SOLEDAD MERCADO

Also called 'Choleng', Soledad Rizal (1870-1929) was the youngest child of the Rizal family.
