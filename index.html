Some Information of Jose Rizal

Jose Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda

Philippine national hero and pride of the Malayan race. In the Laguna town of Calamba, he was born on June 19, 1861.

Francisco Mercado Rizal and Teodora Alonzo y Quintos had 11 children (2 boys and 9 girls)of which he was the seventh.

On June 22, 1861, Rev. Pedro Casanas served as the sponsor for the baptism performed by Rev. Ruffino Collantes in the Calamba Catholic Church.

He created a lot of art include books, paintings, and sculptures that questioned colonial rule and depicted Philippine society's struggles. His passion for art and his desire to inspire change through artistic expression were also clear in his sculptures.
